Malcomines is a Nigerian company based in Jos with interests in mining, food processing, energy, hospitality, and sports. Known for exporting tin and tungsten, it also operates Malco Foods, Malco Resort, Malco Energy, and the award-winning Malco Polo team, while supporting local development and sustainability.
